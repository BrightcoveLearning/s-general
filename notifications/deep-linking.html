Introduction

The Beacon Deep Linking feature allows audiences to either download the Beacon App or to be directed to a specific section or page of the Beacon App. This allows the flexibility to link a viewer to any section of the Beacon app. Deep Linking simplifies how to drive audiences from marketing material or 3rd party sources into their Beacon App experiences to a targeted page. This feature is available for Android and Apple mobile.

For more information on how app notifications work, see Sending App Notifications.

Use Cases

There are two use cases:

  • Deep Link (App Installed)
    • Deep Link is helpful to promote your content.
    • Web App link opens the native app and goes to the corresponding native app page.
    • This works for Apple Universal Links and Android App Links.
  • Deferred deep link (App not Installed)
    • To promote download and installs (iOS and Android Only).
    • The app will send you to the corresponding application store if it is not installed.

Destinations

Supported Deep link destinations (pages, playlists, players):

  • Pages:
    1. Home Page
    2. EPG Pages
    3. Other Landing Pages (top nav items).
  • Assets Details Pages
    1. Movies
    2. Series
    3. Seasons
    4. Episodes
    5. Live Events.
  • VOD Players
    1. Movies
    2. Episodes
    3. Live Events.
  • Live Players
    1. Live Events
    2. Live Channels.
  • Pages as Assets.
  • Collection/Playlist Pages
    1. View All.

This is available for devices iOS / iPadOS and Android Mobile / Tablet.
