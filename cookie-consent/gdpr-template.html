Create the IAB Transparency and Consent Framework 2.2 template.

  1. Click Templates in the left navigation.
  2. Click Add New.
  3. Select the IAB Transparency and Consent Framework 2.2 for the Framework.
  4. Click Next.
  5. Read the IAB TCF information then click I Agree.
  6. Complete the form then click Create Template.
    Note that using GDPR in the template name makes sense as IAB standards are GDPR compliant.
  7. Customize Web Template, Customize CTV Template, Customize Mobile Template.
  8. While still in the Preference Center, open the Advanced Configuration options.
  9. A number of changes must be made here for use with Brightcove Beacon. The changes are listed, then a video follows showing the process how to implement those changes.
    1. Move Social Media Cookies to the Strictly Necessary Cookies subgroup.
    2. Move Targeting Cookies to the Strictly Necessary Cookies subgroup.
    3. Move Functional Cookies to the Strictly Necessary Cookies subgroup.
    4. Move Performance Cookies to the Strictly Necessary Cookies subgroup.
    5. From the Personalised ads and content, ad and content measurement, audience insights and product development group, perform the following:
      1. Remove Create profiles to personalize content as a subgroup, then choose Disable IAB Purpose.
      2. Remove use profiles to select personalized content as a subgroup, then choose Disable IAB Purpose.
      3. Remove Measure content performance as a subgroup, but do NOT disable.
      4. Remove Understand audiences through statistics or combinations of data from different sources as a subgroup, then choose Disable IAB Purpose.
      5. Remove Develop and improve services as a subgroup, then choose Disable IAB Purpose.
    6. For Use precise geolocation data, choose Disable IAB Purpose.
    7. For Actively scan device characteristics for identification, choose Disable IAB Purpose.
    8. For Match and combine data from other data sources, choose Disable IAB Purpose.
    9. For Link different devices, choose Disable IAB Purpose.
    10. For Identify devices based on information transmitted automatically, choose Disable IAB Purpose
  10. Check your configuration by comparing your results to the following, making corrections as needed:
  11. Click Save Template.

Customizing The Web Template

You can customize the layout, styling, content, and behavior of your banner template. This allows you to create a look and feel that matches your organization's branding style and behavior preferences.

  1. After you created your GDPR template, the Template Details page opens to the Banner tab. Click Preference Center.
  2. In the Preference Center:
    1. Open the Styling options
    2. Enable Override Banner Styling
    3. Upload a new logo
      Note: The optimum size for a logo in the Preference Center is: Height: 50px ; Width: 140px
    There is no need to make any other styling changes here as the banner and preference center will automatically be styled based on the design of your Beacon app.
  3. Click Save Template.

Customizing The CTV Template

You can customize the layout, styling, content, and behavior of your banner template. This allows you to create a look and feel that matches your organization's branding style and behavior preferences.

  1. On the COOKIE CONSENT menu, select Templates.
  2. Click on the name of the template you want to customize.
    1. Select the Details tab.
    2. Enable CTV App Template.
    1. Select the Banner tab.
    2. On Platform select CTV App.
    3. On design, Edit the Button Sets with the colors of your Brightcove Beacon Design Guide.
  5. Save and Close
    1. Select the Preference Center tab
    2. On Platform select CTV App.
    3. On Design, Edit General with the colors of your Brightcove Beacon Design Guide.
    4. On Design, Edit Button Sets with the colors of your Brightcove Beacon Design Guide.
  7. Save and Close
  8. Save Template
Note: Every time you make changes to the styling of a template within One Trust, you have to publish the SDK to ensure that all changes are properly implemented and accessible for seamless integration.

Customizing The Mobile Template

You can customize the layout, styling, content, and behavior of your banner template. This allows you to create a look and feel that matches your organization's branding style and behavior preferences.

  1. On the COOKIE CONSENT menu, select Templates.
  2. Click on the name of the template you want to customize.
    1. Select the Details tab.
    2. Enable Mobile App Template.
    1. Select the Preference Center tab
    2. On Platform select Mobile App.
    3. On Design, Edit General with the colors of your Brightcove Beacon Design Guide.
  5. Save and Close
  6. Save Template
Note: Every time you make changes to the styling of a template within One Trust, you have to publish the SDK to ensure that all changes are properly implemented and accessible for seamless integration.
